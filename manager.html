Team Manager Job Description

It would be nice if you...

  • love video games as much as we do
  • are willing to relocate to where we are based for the coming summer
  • can use Prezi and Photoshop/Xara or some other 2D editing tool

Some things you might find yourself doing include:

  • Run / organize weekly team meetings
  • Stay on top of individuals to help us meet deadlines
  • Maintaining a company budget, managing the accounts, and tracking spending
  • Obtaining and maintaining the company’s incorporation license
  • Booking flights and managing/coordinating the team schedule

You might find yourself feeling like a PR rep because we may ask you to...

  • Be in charge of our monthly newsletter
  • Respond to people who are interested in the company/game
  • Update our Facebook, Youtube, and Twitter on a regular basis
  • Update the website with current events
